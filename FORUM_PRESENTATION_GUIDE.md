## ğŸ¯ PRESENTATION BEST PRACTICES

### Before Presenting

#### Preparation (Day Before)
- [ ] Export dashboard to PDF
- [ ] Print one-page summary (backup if tech fails)
- [ ] Rehearse once (10-15 min)
- [ ] Prepare answers to likely questions
- [ ] Test projector/screen sharing

#### Setup (5 Minutes Before)
- [ ] Open tool to Dashboard view
- [ ] Full-screen browser (F11)
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs
- [ ] Silence notifications
- [ ] Test click navigation
- [ ] Have paper copy as backup

### During Presentation

#### Body Language
- **Stand Tall:** Confidence shows strategic clarity
- **Gesture:** Point to specific activities/KPIs
- **Eye Contact:** Engage audience, not just screen
- **Pace:** Speak slowly, pause for impact
- **Energy:** Match enthusiasm to score (88+ = high energy)

#### Voice Techniques
- **Vary Tone:** Emphasize key numbers
- **Pause:** After stating commitments (3 seconds)
- **Project:** Speak to back of room
- **Avoid:** "Um," "like," filler words
- **Emphasize:** Resource %, measurability %, strategic clarity score

#### Screen Navigation
- **Smooth Clicks:** Practice beforehand
- **No Rush:** Let audience absorb visuals
- **Point:** Use cursor/laser to highlight
- **Zoom:** If needed, zoom browser (Ctrl/Cmd +)

### After Presenting

#### Handling Questions
- **Listen Fully:** Don't interrupt
- **Pause:** 2 seconds before answering
- **Refer to Data:** "As you can see here..." (point to screen)
- **Be Honest:** If you don't know, say so
- **Thank Questioner:** "Great question, [Name]"

#### Taking Feedback
- **Note-Taking:** Have notebook ready
- **Non-Defensive:** Feedback = gift
- **Clarify:** "Are you suggesting we should...?"
- **Commit:** "I'll revisit that allocation based on your input"

## ğŸ’¡ ADVANCED TIPS

### For Virtual Presentations (Zoom/Teams)

**Setup:**
- Share screen (full screen, not window)
- Test screen share beforehand
- Mute yourself when not speaking (unless presenting)
- Camera on (if possible) for engagement
- Good lighting on your face

**Engagement:**
- "Can everyone see my screen?" (start)
- Call on people: "John, thoughts on this?"
- Use Zoom reactions for quick feedback
- Share dashboard link in chat

**Technical:**
- Backup: Record presentation as insurance
- Stable internet: Hardwire if possible
- Close bandwidth-heavy apps
- Have phone as backup device

---

### For Large Audiences (20+ People)

**Adjustments:**
- Slow down 20% (larger room = echo)
- Increase font size (Zoom browser to 125%)
- Repeat questions from audience
- Use microphone (don't rely on voice projection)
- Walk around (if stage allows)

**Engagement:**
- "Show of hands: who's struggling with resource focus?"
- "Turn to neighbor: discuss your top activity"
- Strategic pauses (5 seconds)

---

### For Follow-Up Presentations (30/60/90 Days)

**Progress Report Structure:**
1. Original commitments reminder (1 min)
2. Actions completed (2 min)
3. KPI results vs targets (3 min)
4. Course corrections made (2 min)
5. Next 30-day priorities (2 min)

**Show Progress:**
- Green checkmarks on completed actions
- Actual vs target KPI chart
- Resource allocation adherence
- Strategic clarity score change
